Privacy Policy

How Prestige Homes collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Prestige Homes Platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Application Data: Financial, employment, and household information submitted through rental applications.
  • Tour Booking Data: Contact details and scheduling preferences when booking property tours.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, properties viewed, and searches performed, stored locally in your browser for features like "Recently Viewed" and "Saved Listings."

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and improve our services, including processing tour bookings, evaluating rental applications, sending requested communications, and personalizing your browsing experience.

3. Local Storage

This Platform uses your browser's local storage to remember saved listings, recently viewed properties, and preferences such as dismissed banners. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you create an account.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this Platform:

  • Supabase: For account authentication and session management.
  • EmailJS: For transactional emails related to applications and inquiries.
  • RealtyAPI: For property listing data.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the data they process on our behalf.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cryptocurrency Transactions

Cryptocurrency transactions are processed on public blockchains. While wallet addresses are pseudonymous, blockchain transactions are publicly visible and permanent. We do not control or have access to blockchain transaction data beyond what is publicly available.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You can also clear locally stored data (saved listings, recently viewed) at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

8. Children's Privacy

This Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at prestigehomerents@gmail.com.
