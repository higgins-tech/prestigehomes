Accessibility Statement

Prestige Homes is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities.

Last updated: November 2024

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our Platform and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

Accessibility Features

This Platform includes the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • ARIA labels and roles for interactive elements, including navigation, modals, and form controls
  • Full keyboard navigation support, including visible focus states on all interactive elements
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA standards across text and interface elements
  • Descriptive alternative text for property images
  • Form labels and error messages associated with their respective input fields
  • Respect for reduced-motion preferences — animations are minimized for users who have enabled this setting in their operating system
  • Responsive design that adapts to screen readers, magnification tools, and varying viewport sizes

Standards We Reference

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As this Platform evolves to production, we will continue to audit and improve accessibility through automated testing, manual review, and user feedback.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Prestige Homes Platform. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at prestigehomerents@gmail.com and we will make every effort to address your concern.
